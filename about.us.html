Our Story

How we became Kutch's most trusted car rental service

Founded in 2010, Kutch Rentals began with a simple vision: to provide reliable, affordable, and premium car rental services to the people of Kutch and its visitors. What started as a small fleet of 5 vehicles has now grown to become one of the most trusted names in car rentals across the region.

Our journey has been marked by a commitment to customer satisfaction and continuous improvement. We've consistently upgraded our fleet, expanded our services, and trained our staff to ensure we meet the evolving needs of our clients.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of satisfied customers each year, from business travelers to vacationing families, all while maintaining the personal touch that set us apart from the beginning.
