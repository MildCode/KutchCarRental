Our Story

Founded in 2000, Kutch Car Rental began as a small family-owned business with just 2 vehicles. What started as a modest operation to serve local tourists has now grown into Kutch's most trusted car rental service with a fleet of 20+ vehicles.

Our mission has always been simple: to provide reliable, affordable, and comfortable transportation solutions that help visitors explore the breathtaking beauty of Kutch with ease.

Over the past 23 years, we've had the privilege of serving thousands of satisfied customers, from solo travelers to large tour groups. Our deep understanding of the region's unique geography and travel requirements sets us apart.

Whether you're here to experience the magical Rann Utsav, need transportation for corporate events, or want to explore Kutch's rich cultural heritage, we're committed to making your journey comfortable and memorable.
